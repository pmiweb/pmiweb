About Our Institute

Why Choose Pravin More Institute?

Founded and personally supervised by Pravin Sir, our institute is a premier coaching center exclusively for ICSE students in Nashik. With years of experience in ICSE education, we understand the unique requirements of this curriculum and tailor our teaching methods accordingly.

Our institute focuses on building strong conceptual foundations while developing problem-solving skills. We maintain small batch sizes to ensure individual attention for every student.
